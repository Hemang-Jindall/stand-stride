3. Introduction
Overview

Stand & Stride is a mobile-first internship management platform developed to streamline the management of junior internships for students, coordinators, and administrators. The application digitizes the complete internship lifecycle, from student registration to certificate generation, while providing a simple, responsive experience optimized for smartphones.

4. Problem Statement

Currently, internship management relies on spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, manual attendance registers, and paper-based documentation. This creates challenges such as:

Scattered student records
Manual attendance tracking
Delayed certificate requests
Poor communication
Lack of centralized information
Difficulty monitoring internship progress
5. Objectives

The application aims to:

Digitize internship management
Provide a centralized student portal
Simplify attendance tracking
Display internship schedules
Allow online grievance submission
Automate certificate requests
Improve communication through announcements
Reduce administrative workload
